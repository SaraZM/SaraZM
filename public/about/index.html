A little about me

I recently completed my PhD at McGill University, Montreal, Canada. I was a proud member of the first cohort of the Quantitative Life Sciences program at which aims to combine mathematical and computational methods to biological systems at different scales. During my PhD, I worked under the supervision of Dr. Alexandra M. Schmidt applying spatial statistical methods to environmental health problems. My goal is to apply what I have learned so far to help understand and solve problems across different fields, while having a positive impact in people and/or the environment.
